What Is A VPS Used For?

A VPS allows you to host sites (storefront, e-commerce, content, media) and software applications (primarily web: portal, extranet, collaborative solutions, wiki, CRM) with ease.

Unlike shared hosting, it allows multiple applications to be isolated from one another within the same container.

While this VPS container shares physical resources (infrastructure) with other containers, its resources are dedicated to you.

How Do I Upgrade My Vps?

You can upgrade your VPS at any time, directly from your control panel. Your entire system (OS, software, settings) is saved; you don't need to make any changes. Once you select a plan, upgrading happens automatically within minutes.
